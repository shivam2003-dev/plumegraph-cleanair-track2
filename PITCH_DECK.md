# PlumeGraph Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title

**PlumeGraph**  
Neighbourhood pollution intelligence for CleanAir & Clear Streets.

## Slide 2: Problem

City AQI hides street-level harm. Dump fires, construction dust, smog-trap junctions, and industrial plumes can affect thousands of residents while official city AQI looks normal.

## Slide 3: Why Existing Apps Fail

- City stations are sparse.
- Citizen complaints are noisy.
- Satellite data is coarse.
- Municipal alerts rarely prove action worked.

## Slide 4: Solution

PlumeGraph fuses citizen photos, PM sensors, satellite fire/AOD priors, weather, and municipal resources into a live evidence graph.

## Slide 5: AI That Does Real Work

- image smoke/dust/haze scoring
- low-cost sensor anomaly detection
- wind-aware evidence fusion
- 24-hour AQI spike forecast
- cause and confidence explanation

## Slide 6: Municipal Action

The system recommends the right response: mist cannon, cleanup crew, inspection, enforcement, or traffic action.

## Slide 7: Proof Of Action

Every intervention gets a before/after evidence record: PM2.5, PM10, visual haze, citizen complaint trend, and SLA closure.

## Slide 8: Deployability

Prototype runs on GitHub Pages. City pilot maps to Google Cloud Run, Pub/Sub, Cloud SQL/PostGIS, Cloud Storage, BigQuery, Vertex AI, and FCM.

## Slide 9: Inclusion

Cheap Android, offline queue, compressed uploads, Hindi/regional labels, voice notes, privacy blur, and low-literacy-friendly workflows.

## Slide 10: Ask

Pilot PlumeGraph in one ward with 20 low-cost sensors, sanitation fleet participation, ICCC dashboard access, and CSR-supported school/RWA reporting.
